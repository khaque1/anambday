Anam,

when I think of us, the first thought that comes to my mind is the beautiful Hadith of our beloved Prophet ﷺ, as he said:

“Souls are troops collected together and those who familiarized with each other (in the heaven from where these come) would have affinity, with one another (in the world)...”

Our bond is beyond the worldly paradigm of friendship, something from another life. Those who know us know that when there’s Malikah, there’s Anam, and where there’s Anam, there’s no question that Malikah isn’t there. May Allah always keep us united, protect us from the evil eye, and allow us to grow together in our deen and dunya. You are my confidant, best friend, and forever sister. Love YOU more than you know.

Happy birthday, Panam.
